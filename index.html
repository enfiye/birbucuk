birbuçuk

Political Ecology · Collective Archive · 2017–2019

birbuçuk takes its name from the 1.5°C limit of the Paris Agreement. The "half" also marks the in-between space where disciplines, experiences, and voices meet.

Fifteen sessions on water, climate, soil, energy, gender, mining, concrete, gasoline, processors — gatherings of artists, academics, and activists in Istanbul, now translated into seven languages.
